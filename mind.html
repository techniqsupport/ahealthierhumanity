Mind

Molly

Submitted March 3, 2020

I am in my late 20's and developed severe anxiety and mild depression, set off by a trauma, 3 years ago. I had no idea what was wrong with me. I looked up my genetics, I changed my birth control, I just couldn't understand why I shook uncontrollably while my brain went a million miles a minute.

I went to the Dr after a year of suffering, thinking it would go away on it's own, and was diagnosed and put on several different medications for anxiety, depression, and insomnia. I was miserable for the year I took them, feeling so dependent on a medication and not feeling anything like myself. But let's remember, I hadn't felt myself in almost 2 years now.
Breakdown after breakdown because I couldn't understand why it wasn't going back to normal until it clicked, this was my normal now. I had anxiety.

So I did the only thing I could think of. Research. I got on the eastern medicine train, because nature has always been my salvation. I was out in the yard and sunshine constantly, I began receiving acupuncture (THIS WAS THE BIG CHANGE), while meditating and doing yoga also.

I have been completely med free for almost a year and this is the closest to normal I have felt in A LONG TIME. Acupuncture changed my life. It was the first step to healing and reaching for the other parts of eastern medicine that completed me. I have bad days... I always will. But I believe this is my best path.
